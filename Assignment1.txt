Choosing the Front End Technology Stack
Now that you have established the functionalities, aesthetics and identity of your software product together with your client, the next step is to properly choose the technology stack to implement the front end of your application.

Task 0: Based on the requirements you previously discussed with your client, research the market for available free frameworks and technology stacks that will enable you to implement the front end of the application.
Analyse the competitors on the market, devise proper criteria for assessing their quality and benchmark several options. You can present your benchmark in tables such as GPU UserBenchmarks - 453 Graphics Cards Compared.
Justify your technology stack choice during the lab using logical assertions based on the information in the table you provide to your lab teacher.
